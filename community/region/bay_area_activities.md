# Bay Area Family Activities

A curated list of go-to attractions and seasonal outings for families in the Peninsula / South Bay area.
Use this as a suggestion pool when planning weekends — cross-reference season, weather, and kids' current interests.

---

## Amusement

| Attraction | Location | Season | Highlights |
|------------|----------|--------|------------|
| California's Great America | Santa Clara | Spring–Fall | Coasters, Planet Snoopy, FREE Pre-K Pass ages 3–5 |
| Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk | Santa Cruz | Spring–Fall | Classic seaside rides, summer evening laser-light shows |
| Gilroy Gardens Family Theme Park | Gilroy | Late May–Fall | Whimsical "circus trees," gentle rides, Water Oasis (reopens late-May) |

---

## Animals

| Attraction | Location | Season | Highlights |
|------------|----------|--------|------------|
| Oakland Zoo | Oakland | Year-round | Aerial gondola to California Trail, splash-pad at Children's Zoo |
| San Francisco Zoo | SF, Sloat Blvd. | Year-round | Penguin & grizzly feedings, Elinor Friend playground |
| Aquarium of the Bay | SF, Pier 39 | Year-round | Walk-through tunnels with leopard sharks & bat rays; sea-lion docks nearby |
| CuriOdyssey | San Mateo (Coyote Point) | Year-round | Mini-zoo with native CA wildlife, hands-on STEM exhibits |

---

## Beach & Coast

| Attraction | Location | Season | Highlights |
|------------|----------|--------|------------|
| Mori Point Picnic | Pacifica | Spring–Fall (April peak) | Wildflower blooms on coastal bluffs, whale-watching in season |
| Fitzgerald Marine Reserve (tide-pooling) | Moss Beach | Fall–Spring (low tide) | Sea stars, hermit crabs, anemones; pair with Half Moon Bay treats |
| Half Moon Bay beaches | Half Moon Bay | Year-round | Wide sandy beaches, kite flying, sand castle building |

| Attraction | Location | Season | Highlights |

## Museums & Science

| Attraction | Location | Season | Highlights |
|------------|----------|--------|------------|
| Exploratorium | SF, Pier 15 | Year-round | 600+ hands-on science exhibits; member-only Sunday mornings |
| California Academy of Sciences | Golden Gate Park, SF | Year-round | Planetarium, 4-story rainforest dome, "Mysteries of the Deep" exhibit |
| Chabot Space & Science Center | Oakland Hills | Year-round | Telescope viewings, STEAM labs, planetarium shows |
| NASA Ames Visitor Center | Moffett Field, Mountain View | Year-round | Real moon rock, "Journey to Mars" VR, space-flight displays |

## Nature & Hiking

| Attraction | Location | Season | Highlights |
|------------|----------|--------|------------|
| Rancho San Antonio & Deer Hollow Farm | Cupertino | Year-round | Easy family hikes + barnyard goats, sheep, pigs at working farm |
| Coyote Point Recreation Area | San Mateo | Year-round | Bay-front trails, castle-&-dragon mega-playground; CuriOdyssey next door |
| Presidio Tunnel Tops + Outpost Nature Playground | San Francisco | Year-round | 14 acres of lawns, climbing nets, picnic spots, Golden Gate views |
| Wildflower blooms — Mori Point / Edgewood Park | Pacifica / Redwood City | March–May | Peak bloom season; short easy trails |
| Edgewood County Park | Redwood City | Spring (March–May) | One of the best wildflower hikes on the Peninsula |

## Seasonal quick-reference

| Season | Best bets from this list |
|--------|--------------------------|
| **Spring (Mar–May)** | Wildflower hikes (Edgewood, Mori Point), tide-pooling (Fitzgerald), Rancho San Antonio, Coyote Point, Gilroy Gardens opening |
| **Summer (Jun–Aug)** | Boardwalk, Great America, Shoreline Lake, SUP, CAS rainforest, all outdoor venues |
| **Fall (Sep–Nov)** | Oakland Zoo, Half Moon Bay Pumpkin Fest, tide-pooling begins, indoor museums |
| **Winter (Dec–Feb)** | Exploratorium, CAS, Creativity Museum, Chabot, Tahoe snow trip |
| **Rainy day** | Exploratorium, CAS, Creativity Museum, ceramics, glass-blowing, NASA Ames |
