## Cognitive style

**Learning type:** Story-Driven + Visual/Spatial — learns through narrative and
character; also loves hands-on building and making things.

**Key traits:**
> Sam learns best when concepts are wrapped in story or character (e.g., a character
> *wants* to count to 10, not an abstract counting exercise). Loves repetition and
> sing-along formats. Very engaged when the content connects to interests (dinosaurs,
> trains). Hands-on builder — loves Duplo/Lego Duplo and sandbox-style play.
> Attention span for non-story content is short (under 8 minutes).

## Parent notes

> Sam is a typical, happy kindergartener. The goal is to support on-grade skill
> development while feeding curiosity about dinosaurs and building.
> Cocomelon and Super Simple Songs are fine for this age — do NOT flag these as
> junk food. They are age-appropriate and support early literacy/numeracy.
> Main concerns: toy unboxing videos (passive, low vocabulary, no narrative) and
> gaming reaction videos (similarly passive) are eating into quality screen time.
> SEL development is important — Sam is learning to handle disappointment and
> sharing with a new sibling.
